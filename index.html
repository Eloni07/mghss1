Welcome to Mount Gibeon Higher Secondary School,

Where students come first.

Mount Gibeon Higher Secondary School, Tadubi is a private, co‑educational institution located in the rural area of Tadubi, Senapati district, Manipur. Established in 1991, the school is affiliated with the Manipur State Board (BOSEM) for Class 10 and 12 examinations.
