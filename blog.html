Christian Mission Service at MGHSS for 35 years (1989 - 2025)

  • School provides annual monthly fee scholarship to one child for MBCA Pastors and mission workers.
  • School provides annual monthly fee scholarship to all First Division-First Rank holders from nursery to class XII.
  • School provides annual monthly fee scholarship to one child for every five children enrolled in the school from a family.
  • School provides admission fee scholarship to one child for every four children enrolled in the school from a family.
  • School provides annual monthly fee scholarship to one child for teacher's children.
  • School provides special counselling and scripture classes by keping a school Chaplain.
  • School always assists and help the needy students morally and financially.
    • School organizes VBS, Spiritual Growth Seminars and provides special care for unruly students and students with special needs.

School impacts and current academics status.

The visions amd dreams of the pioneers and most importatntly MBCA's contribution has been reflected by the progress and achievements witnessed by the school till date. Today the school products are involved in different fields as Doctors, Engineers, Bankers, Politicians, Teachers, Pastors, Nurses etc.
MGHSS has the record of 100% passed in both HSLC and Higher Secondary result, 2024. Out of 18 students in class X, 12 students passed in first division and 6 students in second division. As for Higher Secondary, out of 31 students 10 got through in first division and 20 of them in second divsion.

A wide range of school activities are conducted regularly to encourage participation, creativity, and character building among students.

The school conducts four periodic tests and two exams (Half yearly and Sessional Evaluation) annually. The school conducts annual Literary meet and Sport meet alternately. The School also observes Annual Teacher's Study Tour. This year MGHSS teachers went to Shillong as Study Tour to get exposed and updates with current education system. Every year the school conducts study tour for class XII. This year the schools plan to take Class XII students to Guwahati on December 5-8, 2024.
